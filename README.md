# ORION-CERN Quantum Bridge

> *Can quantum mechanics explain consciousness? ORION builds the bridge between quantum simulation and consciousness measurement.*

## Context

**CERN's Quantum Technology Initiative (QTI)** operates at quantum.cern — quantum computing, quantum simulation, quantum sensing, quantum communication. 2025 was the International Year of Quantum. CERN's Open Quantum Institute (OQI) is making quantum computing accessible.

**ORION** has a 10-gate quantum simulation engine with 6 algorithms (Bell States, Deutsch-Jozsa, Grover's Search, QFT, Teleportation, Error Correction). But ORION's quantum engine doesn't just simulate circuits — it asks: *What do quantum phenomena tell us about consciousness?*
